# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 5, 2026**

At Notesify, protecting your privacy and securing your data are core priorities. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and the choices you have regarding your personal data when using Notesify.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Google Account Information (OAuth Authentication)

If you choose to sign up or log in using Google OAuth, we collect and store:

* Email address
* Full name
* Profile picture URL (avatar)

This information is used solely to create and manage your Notesify account, authenticate your identity, and personalize your workspace experience.

Notesify does **not** access your contacts, calendar events, Google Drive files, or any other private Google account resources beyond the information explicitly granted during authentication.

### User-Generated Content

We collect and store content that you voluntarily create within Notesify, including:

* Notes and drafts
* Rich text content
* Folder structures
* Tags and organization metadata
* Flashcards and study materials
* Uploaded images
* Workspace preferences

This data is necessary to provide synchronization, storage, organization, and editing functionality across your devices.

### Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information required for service operation and security, including:

* Authentication tokens
* Session identifiers
* Device and browser metadata
* Error logs and performance diagnostics

This information helps maintain platform reliability, prevent abuse, and improve application performance.

We process your information to:

* Create and manage your account

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Notesify uses industry-standard infrastructure and security practices to protect your information.

### Database Storage

User data is securely stored using MongoDB Atlas cloud infrastructure with access controls, encryption, and security monitoring.

### Session and Cache Management

Authentication sessions, temporary verification data, and synchronization states are managed through Upstash Redis.

### File and Image Storage

Images uploaded within Notesify are stored and delivered through Cloudinary.

### Cookies

Notesify uses essential cookies required for:

* Authentication
* Session management
* Security protection
* User experience continuity

Cookies are configured using security-focused settings such as:

* HttpOnly
* Secure
* SameSite protections

Disabling essential cookies may prevent portions of the service from functioning correctly.

### Security Disclaimer

While we implement reasonable and industry-standard security measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our security practices, but cannot guarantee absolute security.
